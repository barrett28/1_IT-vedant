Enhanced Strength

Steve Rogers possesses strength that allows him to perform feats like lifting heavy objects, breaking through barriers, and overpowering numerous foes in combat.

Enhanced Agility and Reflexes

His agility and reflexes are heightened to the peak of human capability, enabling him to dodge bullets, perform acrobatic maneuvers, and engage in complex combat sequences with precision.

Enhanced Stamina and Healing

Captain America's stamina is far superior to that of an average person, allowing him to exert himself for extended periods without fatigue. His healing is also accelerated, enabling him to recover from injuries faster than normal, though not instantaneously like some other superheroes.

Master Combatant and Tactician

In addition to his physical enhancements, Steve is a master hand-to-hand combatant, skilled in various martial arts and armed combat. His indestructible shield, made of vibranium, is both a defensive tool and a weapon, capable of absorbing impacts and being thrown with deadly accuracy.

These abilities, combined with his indomitable will and strategic mind, make Captain America one of the most formidable heroes in the Marvel Universe.
